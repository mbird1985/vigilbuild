Our Story

Vigil Build was born from firsthand experience with the challenges of managing construction operations for power distribution companies and municipalities.

We saw teams struggling with disconnected systems - spreadsheets for inventory, separate software for scheduling, paper forms for maintenance, and endless email chains trying to tie it all together. Critical information was scattered across a dozen different tools, making it nearly impossible to get a clear picture of operations.

When we looked at existing construction management software, we found tools that were either too generic to handle the specific needs of infrastructure work, or so complex that teams gave up trying to use them effectively.

So we built something different. Vigil Build is purpose-built for organizations that manage critical infrastructure - with features designed for the specific workflows, compliance requirements, and operational challenges you face every day.
