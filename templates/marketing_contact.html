Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the demo?

Most demos run 30-45 minutes, though we're happy to spend more time if you have a lot of questions. We focus on the features most relevant to your operations.

Who should attend the demo?

We recommend including key stakeholders who will use or benefit from the platform - operations managers, project managers, fleet managers, and IT when possible.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer pilot programs for qualified organizations. We'll discuss options during your demo based on your needs and timeline.

What about pricing?

Pricing is based on your organization's size and needs. We'll provide a custom quote after understanding your requirements during the demo process.

How long does implementation take?

Most organizations are up and running within 2-4 weeks. We provide guided onboarding and data migration support to ensure a smooth transition.
